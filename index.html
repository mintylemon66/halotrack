A Smart AI-Driven Safety System for Real-Time Emergency Response

HaloTrack helps caregivers respond faster to emergencies with wearable-triggered alerts and live location tracking.

View Demo How it works
Real-World Problem

When every second counts, help is often too slow

A 911 call from a mobile phone can take minutes to triangulate, and victims may not know their exact address or be unable to speak clearly during a crisis. Traditional panic buttons and phone calls assume the person is alert, mobile, and able to reach their device — but during falls, the victims are often incapacitated.

How It Works

Trigger → Alert → Response

HaloTrack helps caregivers respond faster to emergencies with wearable-triggered alerts and live location tracking.

Step 1

Trigger

The wearable necklace detects an emergency — such as a fall — and triggers automatically, without requiring the wearer to act.

Step 2

Alert

A wearable-triggered alert is sent immediately to the caregiver's mobile app, along with live location data.

Step 3

Response

Caregivers see clear, actionable information and the person's live location, so they can respond faster to the emergency.

About the Project

Who, what & why

Who

Demographics living in dangerous areas, such as homeless women.

What

HaloTrack™ is a cross-platform mobile application programmed using Flutter and Firebase. It integrates with a custom wearable device (Arduino-based firmware available in our repo) with an app that makes data clear and accessible via mobile.

Why

Paired with a necklace device, this app makes data clearer, faster, and more actionable on a mobile device that caregivers already carry, in responding when someone may have fallen or gotten attacked in urgent situations.
